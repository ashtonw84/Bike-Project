Huffy Mountain Bike

❮ ❯

The Huffy Rock Creek 24-inch Mountain Bike combines a hardtail frame, great style and the right specs to have a blast over bumps on paved trails or rides around the neighborhood. Smooth 18-speed index shifting system with a Shimano rear indexed derailleur consistently shifts between gears for quick adjustments venturing uphill or accelerating on level ground. Plus, the front suspension fork responds as you ride for better control over curbs, potholes, and branches. The comfortable slim ATB saddle includes an alloy quick-release seat binder for easy adjustments to the right height, or to simply remove when needed. Lightweight aluminum rims and 3-piece steel crank improve performance for a solid ride. Kraton grips add comfort for a great hold while you enjoy the great ride of the Huffy Rock Creek.

  • FEATURE: 18-speed bike's twist shifting system with Shimano rear derailleur delivers consistent response for uphill and downhill rides.
  • Frame and Fork: Hardtail frame and front suspension fork combine for a responsive ride.
  • Speeds: 18-speed mountain bike has consistent twist shifting
  • Brakes: Front and rear linear pull brakes provide consistent, dependable control.
  • Seat: Padded saddle, with durable stitching, can be easily adjusted to the right height.
  • Lightweight aluminum rims improve performance.
  • Kraton grips provide a performance style and comfort feel.
